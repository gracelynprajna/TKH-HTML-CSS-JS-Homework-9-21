Fried Rice Recipe

A delicious blend of veggies, eggs, and rice for a tasty meal for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Oil
  • Rice
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • Yu-Choy
  • 2 Eggs
  • Sweet soy sauce

Steps

  1. Defrost the peas and carrots.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and lightly scramble them.
  3. Wash the yu-choy and then chop them into small pieces.
  4. In a hot pan, pour about 3 tablespoons of sesame oil.
  5. Add your frozen peas and carrots into the pan and add a bit of salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add your cooked rice into the hot pan and lightly sautee on medium heat.
  7. Add your yu-choy onto the pan and mix.
  8. Add your eggs onto the pan and sautee it with the rest of the ingredients.
  9. Add about a tablespoon of sweet soy sauce and mix.
  10. Sautee the mixture for about 5 more minutes on high heat. During this time you can add more salt or pepper to taste.
  11. Transfer your fried rice onto a plate and enjoy!
